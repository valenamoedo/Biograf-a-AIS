Maria Morley: Art Teacher

What motivates this awesome teacher?

Ms. Maria, just like everyone, has a reason, a motif to get up in the mornings. This motivation for her, is being creative in every aspect of her life. She enjoys to make or write stuff. Other things that motivate her is playing music and singing. She´s a very artistic women in every aspect of her life. Another aspect that cannot be left out, is that she is inspired to support her stundents´ creativity.

As a child, did Ms. Maria have a job?

The response to this question is "Yes!". When asked, her answer was very different from the conventional ones and this one was it: "I worked on an island called mackinac island, no cars! only bikes and horses and horse led carriages. I made jewelry and cards and sold them to people in the park." I find this lovely. Seems to be a story out of a book!
